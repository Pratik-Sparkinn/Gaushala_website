About Us

Our mission is to honor Gau Mata by providing a sanctuary dedicated to her care, fostering compassion, and preserving cultural traditions.

Our Story

The Gau Shala was founded in 2005, inspired by traditional values and the importance of cow protection in Hindu culture. Since then, it has provided shelter for over 500 cows, promoting welfare and protection for these sacred animals.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a safe haven for cows, uphold cultural heritage, and foster community engagement. We strive to protect, honor, and nurture every cow within our sanctuary.

Our Journey

  • 2005 - Foundation of the Gau Shala
  • 2010 - Reached 200 cows in care
  • 2015 - Built the on-site Shiv Temple
  • 2020 - Established Green Gym for visitors
