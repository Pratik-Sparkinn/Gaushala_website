Donation Methods

One-Time Donations

Make a single contribution to support the Gau Shala's activities. Your one-time donation can help with daily operations, feed costs, and medical care for cows. Every donation, big or small, brings us closer to our goal of providing a sanctuary for these gentle creatures.

  • ₹500 – Provides one day's feed for a cow
  • ₹1000 – Covers basic medical supplies for a week
  • ₹2000 – Supports the maintenance of our facilities for a month
  • ₹5000 – Contributes to the purchase of new equipment for cows

Donation FAQs

What are the different ways I can donate?

We offer multiple donation options to suit your preferences:

  • One-time donations for immediate support
  • Recurring monthly/quarterly donations
  • Adopt-a-Cow program for personal connection
  • In-kind donations of feed and supplies
How secure is the online donation process?

Your security is our priority. We use:

  • SSL encryption for all transactions
  • Trusted payment gateways
  • Secure data handling protocols
  • Regular security audits
Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, all donations are tax-deductible. You will receive:

  • Official donation receipt
  • Tax deduction certificate
  • Annual donation statement
  • Documentation for tax filing
Can I receive updates on how my donation is used?

We keep our donors informed through:

  • Monthly email newsletters
  • Progress reports and photos
  • Special event invitations
  • Impact stories and testimonials
How do I sponsor a cow?

Sponsoring a cow is easy:

  • Choose your cow from our gallery
  • Select sponsorship duration
  • Complete the sponsorship form
  • Receive regular updates about your cow
What is the refund policy on donations?

Our refund policy includes:

  • Case-by-case evaluation of refund requests
  • Full refund for accidental payments
  • Processing time of 5-7 business days
  • Documentation requirements for refunds
