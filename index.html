Our Mission

Protecting and Honoring Gau Mata

Our mission is to protect and honor Gau Mata, offering shelter, love, and care. Our unique designs for the shelter and natural feeding zones provide a safe haven for these sacred animals.

Our Goals

Save Cow

Focus on rescue and safety for cows in need of protection.

Feed Cow

Ensure a consistent and nutritious diet for their well-being.

Shelter

Provide safe and comfortable shelters for cows to live in.

Good Food

Supply fresh and high-quality food for optimal health.

Medical Care

Provide regular check-ups and treatment by experienced veterinarians.

Preserve Values

Advocate for traditional values and sustainable practices.

Kabutar Khana

Sacred space for pigeons, providing food and shelter in our traditional setup.

Quarantine Shelter

Special care facility for newly rescued or sick cows requiring isolation and treatment.
